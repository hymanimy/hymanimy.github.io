Alex Hyman

Neurosymbolic AI and Formal Verification PhD Student at University of Edinburgh

I am a 1st year PhD student at the University of Edinburgh in the School of Informatics, researching the implementation of neurosymbolic AI models within interactive theorem proving languages. I have a Masters degree in pure mathematics, and so, believe in the formalisation of mathematics in its own right, however, I also care deeply about AI safety and think that 'provable' AI can play an important role in increasing the safety, trustworthiness and even performance of models.
